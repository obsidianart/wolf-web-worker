The field & the sheep
  • The Field has a certain size
  • The field observe the wolf movement and inform the wolf of obstacles and sheep
  • The sheep makes no movement and has no brain
The wolf logic
  • The wolf has no knowledge of the enviroment
  • The wolf can move on any direction and any distance
  • After every movement the wolf get a feedback, i.e. a sheep, an obstacle or just an accepted move
  • If the wolf hit the side he knows that he found an obstacle but not how much he moved (he knows it moved same or less he requested)
The Wolf aim
  • The wolf should eat all the sheeps
  • The wolf should map the enviroment
  • The wolf should explore the entire map
About the code
